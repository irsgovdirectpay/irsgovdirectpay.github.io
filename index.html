IRS Direct Pay is an online portal to make payments online securely from your Bank Account.


How do I make payments directly to the IRS?

  • Type irs.gov/directpay on web browser.
  • Now, select any Payment method.
  • Enter your Personal details - Social Security Number (SSN), date of birth, and address to verify your identity.
  • Then, submit the payment details to IRS . Hit the 'Submit' button.
  • Eventually, finish Payments steps - click 'Submit' button.
